Preparing Yam Porridge


Step-1: Peel, cut and rinse the yam. Ensure that you rinse the yam more than once until the water is clear and no longer slippery. The yam should be in medium-sized chunks about 2 inches thick..

Step-2: Blend your pepper mix. Blend the pepper, tomato, and onion.

Step-3: Boil 2 cups of water till it's hot, add a pinch of salt and add the sliced yam. Boil for 15 minutes.

Step-3: In a smaller pan or pot, pour in your palm oil, onions, blended pepper mix and fry the sauce quicklyfor two minutes.

Step-4: Add your dry fish, blended pepper, salt and seasoning cubes.

Step-5: Pour the sauce into the boiling pot filled with yam and stir it well. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Then let it boil for another 5 to 10 minutes.

Step-6: Take a masher(omorogun in Yoruba) and mash the yams in the porridge.

Step-7: Add your scent leaves and allow to simmer(heat-up) for 2-5minutes or until the porridge is soft with a creamy paste.

Your Porridge is ready, Enjoy!
