Preparing Jollof Rice

tip: Jollof rice has a lot of ingredients so it will be great to have all the ingredients you will be needing in one place


Step-1: In a blender, combine your peppers, tomatoes and onions and add an adequate amount of water in it.

Step-2: Transfer all of the Blended Pepper mix into a Pot and then reduce the heat. Let it simmer for a bit and start stirring occassionally until the mixture is reduced by half. 15 minutes or less.

Step-3: Place your beef or Chicken in a medium bowl with 1 teaspoon curry power and thyme, add a pinch of salt and pepper

Step-4: Heat up the oil you need over a medium heat. Leave the oil on the heat for a bit, add the beef little by little to avoid it from splashing to your skin. After about 7 minutes, remove the meat. And please don't eat it all yet :)

Step-5: In the same containing the oil, add in oil and when the oil shimmers, add in onions, curry powder, your thym, bay leaves and a large pinch of salt and pepper. Keep stirring over a mediu heat until the onion becomes translucent(for about 2 minutes).

Step-6: Add the tomato paste and a bit of oil or 1 spoon of butter until it melts.

Step-6: Add the tomato-pepper mixture to the pot, add in the meat. Raise the heat a bit, cover it up and continue to cook for about 15 minutes.

Step-7: Taste it for seasoning and adjust to your liking. You should know that salt and curry powder should be the most expected taste elements

Step-8: Ensure the rice is washed, add it in and stir it well.

Step-8: Cover it with a foil paper; if you have one and if you don't, cover up the pot. This helps with flavoring .

Step-9: Reduce the heat and cook until the rice and meat are tender, aout 30 minutes is great. Check the rice after 20 minutes. If the pot is dry and a bit of water of water. And stir the mixture to combine.

Step-10: Continue to cook, check every 30 minutes until the rice and meat looks great and tastes great to you.

You can spice it up with more onions and fresh tomatoes. Now you can remove the heat and Enjoy. You deserve it.
