All you will need

  • Semovita

    essential ₦1000.00

    It does not have to be just semovita, you can pick any brand of of your choice.

  • Water

    water Water is Free

    Water is really needed when trying to prepare semo. And ensure its more than enough water.

  • Wooden Spatula

    essential ₦600.00

    It is known as omorogun in Yoruba. And this will be needed for stirring.

  • Heat

    Essential

    The Heat may be in the form of a gas, a stove or a hot-plate. Heat is needed for boiling

Preparing the Semo

funny-tip: This one another meal, keeps disgracing a lot of People when they get it wrong, Do not add too much water, be careful so you don't ruin it.


Step-1: Wash your hands and put all you will be needing in one place.

Step-2: Pour a large amount of water into a clean pot and leave to boil on high heat. It is better to have more Hot water than less.

Step-3: Take a bowl, pour the amount of semovita you will be needing in it and add in cold water into the bowl. Use the wooden spatula to stir the water and semovita continuously until you get a smooth mixture.

Step-4: When the water in the pot is boiling, reduce the heat a bit and then pour the mixed semovita into the boiling water.

Step-5: Continue stirring for 1-2 minutes and then allow it to cook on a low heat for another minute.

Step-6: Stir again until the texture is consistent, and then turn off the heat

Your Semolina should be perfect and ready to serve!. You can enjoy it with Ogbonna soup, Egusi soup, or any other soup of your choice
